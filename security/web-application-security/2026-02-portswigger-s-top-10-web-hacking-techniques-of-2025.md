## Summary

PortSwigger's 19th annual community-powered ranking of the most innovative web security research of 2025, selected from 63 nominations by community vote plus an expert panel. James Kettle's 'HTTP/1.1 Must Die: the desync endgame' was a finalist.

## What to learn

- HTTP desync/request-smuggling research remains at the frontier (HTTP/1.1 Must Die) — _"HTTP/1.1 Must Die: the desync endgame"_
